Latchkeyper

Welcome to Latchkeyper! Between busy work schedules, school, and extracurricular activities, kids and parents don't get much time to talk. Why waste that precious time by asking the same questions over and over? "Have you done your homework?" "Did you clean your room?" "Have you practiced today?" With Latchkeyper, you and your child know exactly what needs to get done and what has already been done. Now you can spend less time nagging and more time talking about more important things.
